48 Hours of Clojure Hacking

Clojure Cup is a distributed programming competition for Clojure and ClojureScript programmers from all over the world, in the spirit of Rails Rumble and Node Knockout.

Your Idea, our Application

You will own the application you build, and can do whatever you want with it after the competition. You can open source your entry if you want, but there will be no requirement to do so.

May The Best Apps Win

After the competition, a panel of expert judges will rank the entries, and their combined rankings will be used to find the winners. In addition to the expert judges, there will be a public vote with a separate award.
